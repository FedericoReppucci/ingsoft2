ACTORS IDENTIFICATION

The actors interacting with our system are the following:

- User: a person who is logged into our system and can use all the services provided
by the application and the car sharing service.

- PowerEnJoy employee: a person who works for the company and, among other things, is responsible 
for the retrieval of cars parked outside of the safe areas.

- Non registered person: a person who is browsing PowerEnJoy's website looking for information on the service
or a person who is using the app but is not yet registered.
